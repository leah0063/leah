  Introduction:


           Osmanthus fragrans is an evergreen tree or shrub belonging to the Oleaceae family. The pedicels are thin and weak, the filaments are very short, and the flowers are extremely fragrant. The fruits are skewed, usually oval, and purple-black in color. The leaves are opposite and leathery, oval or oblong in shape. Or elliptical-lanceolate, with a tapered apex and a wedge-shaped or wide wedge-shaped base. The flowering period is from September to October, and the fruiting period is in March of the following year.





Use value:



         Osmanthus fragrans can be used to treat cough and asthma due to phlegm, cold pain in the epigastrium and abdomen, amenorrhea and dysmenorrhea, etc. "Pharmacology of Traditional Chinese Medicine" records the effectiveness of osmanthus in removing bad breath. "Anhui Chinese Herbal Medicine" and "Zhejiang Medicinal Flora" both record that osmanthus mainly treats stomach cold and abdominal pain, and also has the effect of treating phlegm and cough. In addition to its medicinal value, osmanthus can also be processed into osmanthus cake for consumption. In addition to its therapeutic value, the osmanthus tree also has ornamental value. Its tree shape is upright and elegant, and its soft lines give people a sense of stability and peace.

Sugar Osmanthus

             Sugar Osmanthus is an essential condiment for making osmanthus delicacies. This is the recipe for osmanthus sugar.

Ingredients

  • Appropriate amount of fresh osmanthus
  • Appropriate amount of sugar

Method

  1. Wash the osmanthus with clean water.
  2. Place the drained osmanthus in the microwave on high heat for two minutes.
  3. Take out the sweet-scented osmanthus and stir it, then put it in the microwave on high heat for two minutes, take it out and let it cool.
  4. Take a clean, water-free and oil-free glass bottle and add a layer of white sugar and a layer of osmanthus until it is completely covered.
  5. Tighten the cap of the bottle and place it in a cool place. After the sugar has melted, the sugar osmanthus is ready.
